Set 1 | Summer’s Bedroom

February 9, 2022

•

Liam Walker

•

Posted on Instagram

This set titled “Summer’s Bedroom” was inspired by a last minute idea to take photos that encapsulate my view of summer and its feel. Each self portrait contains a prevalent theme of warm whites and beams of sunlight. These elements carry warmth throughout the images and along side accents of a red shirt and green plant offer more detail and contrast. These three images show, in my opinion, a view of my favorite season: Summer.
